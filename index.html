BREAD MACHINE CINNAMON BUNS
(94)
Ready in - 15min | Serves - 12 persons
made by maria narabovsky
publication: 12/03/2022
Save Recipe
Print Recipe
INGREDIENTS
1/5 Cup 1 Tablespoon 1/4 Teaspoon 1 Tablespoon 1/4 Teaspoon 2 1 Cup 4 Tablespoon 1/3 Teaspoon 1/3 Cup
Flour Sugar Cinnamon Baking powder Salt Eggs Milk Melted Butter Vanilla Chocolate Chips
DIRECTIONS
  • Preheat fry pan (griddle or skillet for Americans).
  • Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, baking powder and salt in a large bowl.
  • Mix together wet ingredients and beat into dry mixture until smooth.
  • Fold in chocolate chips.
  • Pour or spoon batter into fry pan in desired quantity.
  • Flip when top begins to bubble, then cook a minute more.
